BRANDING + IDENTITY

We look to define the essence of your business and the whitespace your brand can occupy in the minds of your consumer.

Through a series of workshops we either devour your brand’s DNA or define it in order to establish the persona that embodies your unique style and vibe that connects directly with your tribe.

We compile everything – your name, brand statements, the products and services you provide, and how you deliver them to create a specific image in your target market’s mind. Once we’ve nailed down how the brand behaves we determine how the brand needs to look, establishing the style, color schemes, typefaces, and overall visual direction.

CONTENT CREATION

Content remains KING. We combine key messaging with visual concepts to create clear, engaging, relevant, useful, searchable, short and sweet – content.

Your content is the life and voice of your brand. We evaluate existing content and communication goals to create meaningful concepts and visual features that are the driving force of your customer experience. The focus is on clearly communicating your brand’s message visually while driving traffic to desired points of content and calls-to-action.

The first step is nailing down a solid strategy, outlining the flow and how we’ll leverage dynamic functionality to deliver the content in a way that supports the overall brand and user experience. From there we craft visual concepts and elements that work into the design and continuously refine throughout the design and development process.
