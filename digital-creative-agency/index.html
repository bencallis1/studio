Get The People is a boutique digital creative agency consisting of talented brand strategists, UX experts, designers, and engineers.

 

Our focus is to create meaningful work that helps brands tell their unique stories visually. It’s about creating experiences that showcase their style and offerings in an authentic way that builds a tribe of beloved customers and fans, and ultimately drives growth.

We love to work with brands that love to shake things up, push the edge, and do something unexpected with their brand and user experience.

Our core areas of expertise include branding, design, ecommerce, user experience design, content development and digital technology – along with a bit of hand holding, free hugs, the occasional but necessary brutal slap of honesty, and lots of laughs.

OUR APPROACH

Each project has it’s own unique life so we start fresh each time.

 

Leveraging an intuitive process and a clear understanding of our clients and their audiences is the groundwork for solving their communication and design challenges.

Above all, we look to achieve that perfect balance in everything we do – simple but dynamic, progressive yet refined, always striving to create the best brand experience possible.

A deep understanding of business, art, ecommerce, media, fashion and industry trends, coupled with an obsession for beautiful design, the creative world and where it’s going, is what we bake into every project.

THE STORY SO FAR

Get The People opened it’s doors as a design agency in 2011 – but that’s not where the story begins.

CIRCA 2001

Let’s rewind. A dozen years ago, Get The People was nothing more than a concept for an art campaign. It began with an obsession of art and graphics – combined with a desire to spread and share new concepts with the world. The words were plucked mid-sentence during a subway conversation, and for years the words Get The People were sprayed, inked, stamped, stenciled, and stickered all over the streets of New York City.

Today after years of visibility, Get The People lives on. The name can still be found on the streets of NYC (legally now), on the window of our design studio located in Manhattan’s Lower East Side. Together Rad and Megan run GTP, partnering with local development and digital marketing shops, providing full scope creative and ecommerce services to retail, fashion, luxury and entertainment brands.

We live for those awesome moments where smart strategy and solid execution meet happy clients.
